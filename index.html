How to Play

Objective

Clear the minefield without detonating any mines.

Controls

Desktop:

  • Left-click: Reveal a cell
  • Right-click: Place/remove a flag
  • Both buttons: Chord (reveal adjacent cells if flags match)

Mobile:

  • Tap: Reveal a cell
  • Long press: Place/remove a flag

Numbers

Numbers indicate how many mines are adjacent to that cell.

Power-ups

Special cells grant abilities when revealed:

  • Shield: Survive one mine hit
  • Detector: Briefly reveals a mine location
  • Time Freeze: Pauses the timer
  • Safe Reveal: Reveals safe cells automatically
